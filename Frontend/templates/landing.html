Rhetoric Bot

The purpose of this research is to explore comfortable web environments to talk about issues that would be otherwise too uncofortable to talked about with others, specially others who share dissimilar beliefs as us. A chatbot could provide a private channel to talk about heated topics to a grounded entity that is not going to try and provoke you or just echo your sentiment back at you.
